# SCC Public Sector AI Incident Analysis

This version is simplified for product planning and stakeholder discussion.

It shows:

- the existing FixMyStreet fork as one bounded product system
- the main product areas inside that system
- the AI and automation layer extending from the specific areas it supports
- the business outcomes expected from those additions

This diagram is intentionally less technical than the engineering version. It focuses on where AI creates product value, where the new AI layer connects to the current platform, and which technologies are involved.
