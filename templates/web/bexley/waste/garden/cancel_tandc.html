Right to cancel

You have the right to cancel this contract within 14 calendar days without giving any reason.

To exercise the right to cancel, you must inform us either by:

  1. Online form - complete the cancellation process by submitting your cancellation request below
  2. Email - send an email to Bexley's Garden Waste department using the details included below
Please ensure that you include your full name, your address, a contact telephone number and an email address. Upon receipt of your cancellation request, we will communicate to you an acknowledgement of receipt of such a cancellation without delay.

Effects of cancellation, including reimbursement (if applicable)

If you decide to cancel the service during the cancellation period, you will automatically be issued with a full refund. However, if you decide to cancel the service after the cancellation period, no reimbursement will be provided (unless we have breached any terms of this contract).

To meet the cancellation deadline, you are required to send notification of your decision to cancel, ensuring that it is received by us (The London Borough of Bexley) before the cancellation period has expired.

We will make the reimbursement without undue delay, and not later than 14 days after the day on which we are informed about your decision to cancel this contract.

We will make the reimbursement using the same means of payment as you used for the initial transaction, unless you have expressly agreed otherwise.
