[% IF c.cobrand.moniker == 'hackney' %]

If the problem persists, you can also complete diary sheets so we can look in depth at the times and extent of the problem.

Should the noise occur between 6.30pm and 02:00am on a Thursday, 9.00pm and 02.00am on a Friday / Saturday, or 6.30pm and 02.00am on Sunday, please contact the out of hours team on 020 8356 4455. If the noise is ongoing, we will visit you to try and witness the nuisance (other priorities permitting) and if necessary, take action to stop it.

[% END %]

If the noise occurs at any other time please use this site and check the "I have reported this before" option at the start of the process. Out of hours monitoring visits can be arranged if additional reports show that the noise is regularly occurring outside our office hours. If, at any time we witness noise that amounts to a statutory nuisance, then a noise abatement notice would normally be served on the person responsible. If the noise continues after that, a prosecution would be considered, if we can prove that the notice has been breached. Please note: without witnessing the nuisance, there is only limited action we can take.

If you have provided your contact details, we will keep you updated on progress. If appropriate we will update you on our planned course of action.
