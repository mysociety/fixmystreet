Before you make a request

  • Requesting a sharps box collection or delivery usually takes around 5 minutes
  • Please make sure you have read the Clinical Waste Collections page on our website
  • Before confirming your request, you need to check all the information provided is correct
  • You need to provide the following information:
    • number and size of sharps boxes that need to be collected or delivered
    • where the boxes will be located (if you are requesting a collection)
    • your name, address and contact information

Don't use this service to:

  • request a sharps box delivery or collection for a business or commercial address
  • request a sharps box delivery or collection for sharps that are produced by a healthcare practitioner who visits your home to treat you
  • request a new 'orange bag' collection for dressings, swabs etc.
