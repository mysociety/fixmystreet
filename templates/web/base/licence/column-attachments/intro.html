This form is to apply for a column attachments licence on the Transport for London Road Network (TLRN)

Before you start

[% INCLUDE 'licence/intro_documents.html' %]

You will need:

  • Public Liability Insurance certificate, with minimum cover of £10 million
  • Map showing the location of each of the column attachments
  • Risk Assessment Method Statement (RAMS)
  • Structural Testing (if required for your installation):
    • Design Calculations (CD354 – Design of Minor Structures)
    • Onsite Asset Load Testing aligned with BS EN 40
      • $
    • Design and Check Certificate (Appendix J of CG 300)
