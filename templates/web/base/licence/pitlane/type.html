Based on your selections, the below Pit Lane type will be applicable. A Pit Lane (Major) is required if any of the following apply.

  • The duration of the Pit Lane is 28 weeks or longer
  • Phased traffic management schemes where each phase is less than 28 weeks, but the combined total of all phases exceeds 28 weeks
  • There is likely to be significant impact to the network due to a reduction in lane capacity in a traffic-sensitive location
  • One of more bus service alterations are required
