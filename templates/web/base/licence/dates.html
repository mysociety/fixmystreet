The processing periods for applications vary depending on the impact the activity will have on the road network. The following are the minimum advance notice periods required for giving approval to an application:

  • Activities with low or minimal network impact: 4 weeks before start date
  • Activities with moderate impact or require a suspension: 8 weeks before start date
  • Activities with high impact or require a lane closure: 12 weeks before start date
  • Activities with high impact and/or requiring a Traffic Order: 16 weeks before start date
