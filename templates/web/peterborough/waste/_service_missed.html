Our crews record bins as not collected for one or more of the following reasons:

  • Overweight – The bin was too heavy for safe lifting.
  • Contaminated – The bin contained items that should not be placed in that bin.
  • Not presented – The bin was not out when the crew arrived.

Bins recorded for any of these reasons will not be collected untill your next scheduled collection day. Please ensure that:
